Project Aura is XREAL's flagship augmented reality headset running Android XR. Revealed at CES 2026, it represents a significant leap for XREAL, moving from tethered smartphone accessories to a standalone (or wirelessly tethered) spatial computing platform with a massive 70° field of view.

## Overview

XREAL Project Aura uses optical see-through technology, allowing users to see digital content overlaid directly onto the real world without the use of cameras for passthrough. This differentiates it from the Samsung Galaxy XR, which is a closed mixed-reality headset.

## Technical Specifications

- **Field of View:** 70° Horizontal (Industry-leading for AR glasses).
- **Operating System:** Android XR (Native).
- **Input:** Hand tracking, eye tracking, and 6DoF head tracking.
- **Form Factor:** Standalone glasses with optional wireless compute pack.

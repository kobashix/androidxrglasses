The partnership between Gentle Monster and Google targets the premium, fashion-forward segment of the Android XR smart glasses market. Known for their avant-garde designs and high-end retail experiences, Gentle Monster is developing specialized frames for the Android XR platform.

## Fashion-First Design

Gentle Monster's involvement ensures that Android XR glasses aren't just technical devices, but also high-fashion accessories. Their designs often push the boundaries of traditional eyewear, making them a key partner for Google's luxury-segment strategy.

## Global Retail Presence

With flagship stores in major cities worldwide (Seoul, Shanghai, New York, London), Gentle Monster provides an international premium retail channel for the Android XR ecosystem, complementing Warby Parker's mass-market focus in the US.

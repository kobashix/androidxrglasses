The partnership between Warby Parker and Google represents the primary mass-market strategy for Android XR smart glasses. With a $150M investment from Google, Warby Parker is developing a line of stylish, affordable eyewear that integrates Android XR technology natively.

## Mass-Market Strategy

By partnering with Warby Parker, Google aims to overcome the "glasshole" stigma that plagued Google Glass. Warby Parker's established retail presence and focus on fashionable frames provide the perfect channel for everyday consumers to adopt smart eyewear.

## Google's $150M Investment

Google's investment consists of $75M upfront and a further $75M in equity stake tied to specific commercial milestones. This deep financial integration ensures that Warby Parker is the preferred retail partner for the first wave of consumer Android XR glasses in 2026.

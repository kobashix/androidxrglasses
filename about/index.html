About AndroidXRGlasses.com

We are an independent publication dedicated to becoming the world’s most authoritative source of news and reference information about Android XR glasses, headsets, and the broader extended reality platform built by Google and its partners.

Our Mission

Android XR represents a meaningful shift in personal computing — a platform that could make AI-assisted glasses as ubiquitous as smartphones. We believe that transition deserves dedicated, accurate, independent journalism and encyclopedic documentation.

AndroidXRGlasses.com exists to track every announcement, every device specification, and every partner relationship in the Android XR ecosystem — and to make that information accessible to anyone who needs it: consumers, developers, journalists, and industry professionals.

What We Cover

  • Breaking news on Android XR hardware, software, partnerships, and market developments
  • Wiki entries for every device, technology, and company in the Android XR ecosystem
  • Device specifications — verified, complete, and regularly updated
  • Analysis of competitive dynamics between Android XR, Apple Vision Pro, and Meta’s XR products
  • Developer resources — coverage of the Android XR SDK, APIs, and developer program

Editorial Independence

AndroidXRGlasses.com is not affiliated with, funded by, or editorially controlled by Google LLC, Samsung Electronics, or any other company in the Android XR ecosystem. We do not accept sponsored content, paid reviews, or undisclosed affiliate relationships. Our editorial decisions are made independently.

If we publish a review, analysis, or opinion, it reflects our editorial team’s honest assessment. See our full Editorial Policy for sourcing standards, correction procedures, and author credential requirements.

Authors & Credentials

Articles are authored by staff reporters and contributors with backgrounds in technology journalism, XR development, and consumer electronics. Author credentials, publication date, and last-verified timestamps are displayed on every article.
